Main Characters

Tanjiro Kamado

Age: 15

Role: Protagonist

Background: A kind-hearted young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon. Tanjiro is determined to find a cure for Nezuko and to rid the world of demons.

Skills: Mastery of Water Breathing techniques and later learns techniques from the Sun Breathing style.

Nezuko Kamado

Age: 14 (as a human, but appears as a demon with a perpetual youthful appearance)

Role: Tanjiro’s younger sister and a demon

Background: Despite being a demon, she retains her human emotions and fights alongside Tanjiro. Her goal is to find a cure for her demon condition.

Skills: Enhanced physical abilities and the ability to use Blood Demon Art, including controlling fire and regenerative abilities.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Age: 16

Role: Tanjiro’s companion and fellow demon slayer

Background: A cowardly but powerful fighter who wields lightning-based techniques when unconscious or under extreme stress.

Skills: Mastery of Thunder Breathing techniques.

Inosuke Hashibira

Age: 15

Role: Tanjiro’s companion and fellow demon slayer

Background: A brash and fierce fighter who wears a boar's head as a mask and uses beast-based combat techniques.

Skills: Mastery of Beast Breathing techniques.

Kanao Tsuyuri

Age: 16

Role: Demon slayer and Tsuguko (apprentice) of Shinobu Kocho

Background: A highly skilled fighter who uses the Flower Breathing techniques and has a calm, stoic demeanor.

Skills: Mastery of Flower Breathing techniques.

Upper Moon Demons

Kokushibo (Upper Moon 1)

Age: Over 1,000 years old (as a demon)

Background: Former human named Michikatsu Tsugikuni, older brother of Yoriichi Tsugikuni. Became a demon due to his desire for power and immortality.

Skills: Mastery of Moon Breathing techniques and enhanced physical abilities.









Doma (Upper Moon 2)

Age: Over 100 years old

Background: A demon with a sadistic nature who enjoys consuming humans. Originally a human who became a demon seeking immortality.

Skills: Mastery of Ice Breathing techniques and manipulation of ice-based attacks.

Akaza (Upper Moon 3)

Age: Over 100 years old

Background: A former martial artist who became a demon after being turned by Muzan. He values strength and challenges.

Skills: Mastery of Blood Demon Art related to martial arts.

Hantengu (Upper Moon 4)

Age: Unknown

Background: A demon who can create multiple manifestations of himself, each with different abilities.

Skills: Ability to create and control various forms of himself, each with unique powers.

Gyokko (Upper Moon 5)

Age: Over 100 years old

Background: A grotesque demon with fish-like abilities. Enjoys collecting and displaying human heads.

Skills: Mastery of Blood Demon Art related to fish and water manipulation.

Kaigaku (Upper Moon 6)

Age: Unknown (younger than Kokushibo)

Background: Former apprentice of Kokushibo who became a demon. Uses Thunder Breathing techniques.

Skills: Mastery of Thunder Breathing techniques and enhanced combat abilities.

Lady Tamayo and Her Servants

Lady Tamayo

Age: Over 100 years old (as a demon)

Background: A former demon who seeks to find a cure for demons and helps Tanjiro and his companions.

Skills: Deep knowledge of demon biology and unique abilities related to her research.

Yushiro

Age: Unknown (younger than Tamayo)

Background: Tamayo’s loyal assistant and a demon. Helps with research and supports Tanjiro and his group.

Skills: Ability to create illusions and assist in combat.

Hashira (Pillars)

Giyu Tomioka (Hashira of Water)

Age: 21

Background: Known for his stoic nature and exceptional skill with Water Breathing techniques. He is a dedicated and skilled fighter.

Skills: Mastery of Water Breathing techniques.

Shinobu Kocho (Hashira of Insect)

Age: 18

Background: A skilled and intelligent fighter using Insect Breathing techniques. Driven by a desire to find a cure for demons after losing her family.

Skills: Mastery of Insect Breathing techniques and poison-based combat.

Tengen Uzui (Hashira of Sound)

Age: 23

Background: A flamboyant and charismatic fighter known for his Sound Breathing techniques. Protects his village and strives to make a significant impact.

Skills: Mastery of Sound Breathing techniques.

Rengoku Kyojuro (Hashira of Flame)

Age: 20

Background: Known for his passionate and heroic demeanor. The Flame Hashira who embodies courage and dedication, prominently featured in the “Mugen Train” arc.

Skills: Mastery of Flame Breathing techniques.

Mitsuri Kanroji (Hashira of Love)

Age: 19

Background: Known for her exceptional strength and compassionate nature. Struggles with her own strength and acceptance while protecting others.

Skills: Mastery of Love Breathing techniques.

Sanemi Shinazugawa (Hashira of Wind)

Age: 21

Background: Aggressive and harsh, with a tragic past involving the loss of his family to demons. Uses Wind Breathing techniques.

Skills: Mastery of Wind Breathing techniques.

Obanai Iguro (Hashira of Snake)

Age: 21

Background: Known for his strict and disciplined nature. Uses Snake Breathing techniques and has a unique fighting style based on serpentine movements.

Skills: Mastery of Snake Breathing techniques.

Muichiro Tokito (Hashira of Mist)

Age: 15

Background: A young and prodigious fighter with a mysterious and aloof demeanor. Uses Mist Breathing techniques to obscure his movements and attack opponents.

Skills: Mastery of Mist Breathing techniques.

Gyomei Himejima (Hashira of Stone)

Age: 23

Background: Known for his immense strength and unwavering faith. The Stone Hashira who uses powerful Stone Breathing techniques.

Skills: Mastery of Stone Breathing techniques.

Master

Kagaya Ubuyashiki

Age: 23 (due to his illness, appears older)

Background: Leader of the Demon Slayer Corps, known for his wisdom and strategic insight. Provides guidance and support to the demon slayers despite his terminal illness.

Skills: Strategic leadership and organizational skills.
